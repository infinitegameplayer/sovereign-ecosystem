# Optional Path - Lightweight Business or Life Operating Surface

Purpose: create a simple recurring operating surface for practical life or business realities without adding heavy new architecture.

This path is useful when the ecosystem is ready to hold more lived reality, but you do not want to trigger a full module install or a giant structural build.

## When This Path Fits

This path is applicable if:
- you want a practical home for recurring real-world concerns
- there is a life or business lane that keeps showing up but still feels too vague
- you want a light operating surface before deeper structure is added

Default next move:
- convert this path into a Pending Plan if it applies
- let the system hold it until the right first shape becomes clearer

## What This Path Does

This path creates a lean operating surface for something practical:
- business operations
- personal logistics
- recurring planning
- a repeating life-management lane

The point is not to over-design it.
The point is to give recurring reality somewhere to land.

## Good Questions For This Path

- What recurring part of life or work wants a home?
- What is currently too important to stay vague but too small for a giant build?
- What would the simplest useful operating surface look like?

## Completion Signal

This optional path is complete when:
- the recurring lane has a simple first home
- it is clear what belongs there
- the surface is useful enough to return to without needing deeper architecture immediately
