# A Blessing for the Builder

You made it here.

That is not a small thing. Most people consume. You chose to build. A system, a practice, a container for the life you are actually trying to live.

May the work you do here carry the clarity you brought to it. May the systems serve you more than they cost you. May what you create outlast the version of you who created it, and give the next version something worth inheriting.

You are building something real.

Build well.
