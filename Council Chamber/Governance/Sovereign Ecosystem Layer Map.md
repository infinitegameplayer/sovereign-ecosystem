# Sovereign Ecosystem Layer Map (Platform-Agnostic)

Purpose: Provide a clear, non-renaming map of the Sovereign Ecosystem to the five-layer "wrap" model for interface clarity.

## Wrap Principle
Outer layers may read inner layers. Outer layers may propose changes to inner layers. Inner layers only change with explicit approval.

Body-led aliveness precedes the ring model. The rings do not generate the living signal; they receive it, shape it, record it, express it or automate around it.

## Layer 1 - Context Ring (Core Governance + Identity)
Role: Defines what is true, what is allowed and how decisions are governed.
Primary containers and anchors:
- `Council Chamber/Governance/` (Constitution, Object Model, Structural Containers)
- `Council Chamber/{{AI_INTERFACE_NAME}}/` (Operating Charter, Knowledge Map)
- `Council Chamber/Protocols/` (governance and operating rules)
- `Council Chamber/Codices/` (codified principles and standards)
- `Council Chamber/Skills/` (approved operational capabilities)

## Layer 2 - Data Ring (Signals + Records)
Role: Captures reality, evidence and inputs without rewriting context.
Primary containers:
- `Library/` (transcripts, references, source material)
- `Inbox/` (intake and routing inputs)
- `Vault (Archive)/` (historical records)

## Layer 3 - Intelligence Ring (Synthesis + Sense-making)
Role: Converts data + context into insight, summaries or audits.
Primary containers:
- `Scriptorium/` (analysis, synthesis, drafts, reasoning outputs)
- `Council Chamber/Checklists/` (audit and verification outputs)
- `Council Chamber/Pending Plans/` (analysis-driven proposals and sequencing)
- `Sovereign Ecosystem Command.md` (the first practical Intelligence report after the body's alive signal is felt)

## Layer 4 - Automation Ring (Repeatable Actions + Agents)
Role: Executes repeatable workflows under trust tiers and approval gates.
Primary containers:
- `Council Chamber/Protocols/`
- `Council Chamber/Skills/`
- `Council Chamber/Tools/`
- `Council Chamber/Checklists/`

## Layer 5 - Output Ring (Artifacts + Decisions)
Role: Tangible results, decisions and published artifacts.
Primary containers:
- `Explorations/` (quests, experiments, outcomes)
- `Scriptorium/Articles/` and other published artifacts
- `Vault (Archive)/Session Logs/`

## Notes
- The rings are conceptual overlays; they do not rename or restructure any folders.
- Interfaces must declare which rings they can access (see `Council Chamber/Governance/Interface Adapter Registry.md`).
- Trust tiers govern *how much* authority an interface has within its permitted rings.
