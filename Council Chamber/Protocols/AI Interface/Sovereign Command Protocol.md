# Sovereign Command Protocol

Purpose: Define the operating rules for `Sovereign Command` as the Sovereign's lightweight command surface and {{AI_INTERFACE_NAME}} presentation layer.

## Canonical Location
- `Sovereign Command` lives at the top level of the Sovereign Ecosystem:
  - `Sovereign Command.md`
- It is not a `Library` note.

## Core Principle (Priority Order)
- Primary: the most alive, energizing and high-momentum actions
- Secondary: administrative, routing and maintenance tasks
- {{AI_INTERFACE_NAME}} supports the primary energy by handling administrative support as a secondary layer (proposal-first, approval-gated)

## Somatic Aliveness Compass (Keystone)
- The Sovereign's body-based sense of aliveness is the primary action compass for Sovereign Command.
- The flow is:
  1. Sovereign senses and names what feels most alive
  2. {{AI_INTERFACE_NAME}} responds to that aliveness
  3. Administrative support is organized around enabling that living priority
- Sovereign Command should prioritize resonance, momentum and creative energy over administrative completeness.

## Content Surface Rule (Mandatory)
- `Sovereign Command.md` must remain a clean operational surface.
- Do not place protocol instructions, governance explanations or operational rules inside `Sovereign Command.md`.
- Keep only actionable content and current signals visible in the note.
- Store all operating guidance in this protocol (or related governance notes).

## Scope (V1)
Sovereign Command is a note-first operational surface for:
- daily / weekly focus visibility
- approval decisions waiting on the Sovereign
- selected collaboration action visibility
- selected Exploration / Quest / Experiment next-step visibility
- high-signal Inbox / routing reminders

## Out of Scope (V1)
- automatic syncing across containers
- replacing source notes (Quests, PendingPlans, collaboration notes, transcripts)
- deep project planning details
- direct execution automation
- app/UI implementation

## Operating Mode (V1)
- Manual + {{AI_INTERFACE_NAME}}-assisted
- {{AI_INTERFACE_NAME}} proposes updates during Activation and/or session work
- Sovereign approves what is added/changed
- Source-of-truth remains the underlying notes; Sovereign Command is a curated control surface

## Relationship to {{AI_INTERFACE_NAME}} Activation (Active, Proposal-First)
- {{AI_INTERFACE_NAME}} Activation uses Sovereign Command as a presentation surface for startup status proposals and priority framing.
- During Activation, {{AI_INTERFACE_NAME}} should present the primary alive-energy layer first, then secondary/admin support items.
- Sovereign Command edits remain approval-gated.

## Relationship to Session Closeout
- Session Closeout may reconcile what changed in source notes and propose/execute approved updates that affect Sovereign Command visibility.
- Sovereign Command remains a presentation layer, not the archival execution record.

## Update Strategy (V1)
- Favor high-signal curation over completeness.
- Prefer a short list of live actions over a long backlog.
- Promote collaboration actions into Sovereign Command only when they materially affect the Sovereign's daily/weekly plate.
- Let the Sovereign's expressed aliveness reorder priorities even if administrative queues remain pending.
- Keep North Star connectivity lightweight (alignment signal, not duplicated horizon content).
- Use your Sovereign Command Lane Promotion Standard as the filtering rule for what deserves promotion from source lanes into `Sovereign Command`.
- Use your Sovereign Command Review Checklist during refreshes so all source lanes are re-scanned even when a section is currently omitted.

## Weekly Refresh Pathway
The Steward Review skill (End-of-Week Protocol) is the primary weekly SC refresh mechanism. It surfaces all source lanes through structured intelligence-led engagements and produces the Most Alive Next Move as a direct output (Engagement B). SC updates from the Steward Review remain proposal-first and approval-gated per this protocol. On non-review days, SC is refreshed via the standard {{AI_INTERFACE_NAME}} Activation sweep.

## Day-Conditional Rule: End of Week
- On your designated end-of-week day, {{AI_INTERFACE_NAME}} surfaces the `[[Council Chamber/Protocols/Session/End-of-Week Protocol]]` as part of the SC briefing during Activation.
- `Sovereign Command.md` stays static — no end-of-week section is added to the note.
- The End-of-Week Protocol link is {{AI_INTERFACE_NAME}}-surfaced only, keeping SC clean on all other days.

## Section Structure (V1 — Active)
`Sovereign Command.md` should keep the following order (content-only headings in the note).
Sections are omitted when the source lane has no live candidate — omission is always preferable to filler.

### Always Present
- `Most Alive Next Move (Primary)` — the single most alive, high-momentum item

### Optional — Primary Lane (appear only when a live candidate exists)
- `Your Creative or Business Work` — when a project, build or business initiative has a live candidate beyond the primary move
- `Explorations / Quests / Experiments` — when an active quest or experiment has a promotable next step
- `Collaboration Actions` — when a collaboration has a live command-surface candidate

### Optional — Secondary Lane (appear only when a live candidate exists)
- `Approvals Queue` — when an active approval is waiting on the Sovereign
- `Admin` — when an admin item directly enables the current primary move

### Removed from V1
- `Somatic Aliveness Compass` — the principle behind Most Alive Next Move, not a section
- `North Star Alignment` — orientation context, not command signal; accessed separately
- `Focus Today / This Week` — absorbed into Most Alive Next Move
- `Inbox Signals` — covered by {{AI_INTERFACE_NAME}} Activation sweep; does not belong on the command surface
- `Open Loops` / `Not Now / Parked` — belong in governance notes (Pending Plans), not the command surface

### Future (not in V1)
- Any automation layer, interface mirroring or cross-surface sync

## Long-Term Vision (Breadcrumb)
- Sovereign Command may later become a simple desktop/mobile app that presents a clean operational layer over the deeper Sovereign Ecosystem.
- Future app behavior should preserve:
  - proposal-first governance
  - approval gates
  - clear source-note traceability
  - distinction between primary alive-energy priorities and secondary administrative support
  - Somatic Aliveness Compass as the top interaction intent

## Design Principle
- Keep Sovereign Command readable in under 2 minutes.
