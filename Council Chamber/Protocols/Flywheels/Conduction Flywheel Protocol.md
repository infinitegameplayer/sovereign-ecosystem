# Conduction Flywheel Protocol

Purpose: Refine archetypal conduction signals from Quest completion and feed governance updates before Vault archival.

Scope: Completed Quests and Experiments that include Trifecta Reflection and Archetypal ROI.

Governed by: your Flywheel Codex of the Sovereign

## Flywheel Loop
1. Quest Completion Capture
   - Ensure Trifecta Reflection + Archetypal ROI are recorded.
2. Conduction Synthesis
   - Summarize Subconscious / Digital / Physical signals.
   - Extract archetype + domain signals (provisional).
   - When visible, note strengthened archetypal energies, exercised stewardship domains and any emerging contribution or wealth signals.
3. Governance Map Proposal
   - Propose updates to `Council Chamber/Governance/Archetypal Governance Map.md`.
   - Flag new growth edges or integrations.
4. North Star Bias Signal (optional)
   - If repeated patterns emerge, propose an Energetic Emphasis line.
5. Vault Handoff
   - Proceed with Universal Vault Flywheel after approval and proposal review.

## Triggers
- Quest status changes to `completed`.
- Session Closeout detects completed Quests or Experiments with Trifecta Reflection present.
- Universal Vault Flywheel intake includes Quest artifacts.
