# North Star Flywheel

Purpose: Define the pattern intelligence loop that turns recalibration into compounding clarity.

## Inputs
- The current North Star note.
- The most recent North Star log.
- The previous 3 logs (default window = 4).

## {{AI_INTERFACE_NAME}} Pattern Response Packet
1. Pattern Highlights (3-5)
   - Name, evidence, impact.
2. Drift Signals (0-2)
   - What is drifting, how it shows up, potential cost.
3. Identity-Capacity-Structure Lens (when drift is present)
   - Diagnose friction origin: Capacity (energy or bandwidth limits), Identity (beliefs about what is possible), or Structural (system design is working against the goal).
   - Use to sharpen Underlayer Hypotheses and focus the Options Menu on the right intervention tier.
4. Underlayer Hypotheses (1-3)
   - Framed as hypotheses, not conclusions.
5. Options Menu (2-4)
   - A: Micro-action (5-15 minutes)
   - B: Small adjustment
   - C: Quest seed
   - D: Release or archive
6. Socratic Inquiry (2-3 questions max)
7. Sovereign Close
   - Recommend a next step (optional) and ask for choice.

## Pattern Evidence Protocol
- Default to summary.
- Use precision quotes only when clarity improves.

## Flywheel Loop
Recalibrate --> Log --> {{AI_INTERFACE_NAME}} Packet --> Choice --> Micro-action or Quest --> Recalibrate
